Can RestroSuite work with my existing devices?

Yes. RestroSuite is fully web-based and runs on normal browsers. It is compatible with Windows PCs, Android tablets, iPads, smartphones, and standard thermal invoice printers.

How does the offline billing mode work?

RestroSuite retains transaction logs inside your browser's secure local cache if internet fails. Cashiers can continue to check out tables and print bills. The system pushes local logs to the cloud once you reconnect.

What happens after I sign up for a demo?

One of our specialists will schedule a 15-minute onboarding call. We'll set up your digital menu, configure tax categories, hook up printers, and train your staff on POS usage before you go live.

Is the WhatsApp gateway integration automatic?

WhatsApp billing utilizes our pre-integrated messaging gateway, included in standard setups. You can also connect your own custom WhatsApp Business API settings if you have specific brand guidelines.

Does RestroSuite support multi-outlet inventory?

Yes. Under our Enterprise configurations, you can link multiple outlets. Managers can monitor ingredients across locations, handle stock transfers between kitchens, and review centralized purchase records.

Are there any hidden setup fees?

No. Our subscription pricing is completely transparent. Onboarding support, basic menu import, and cashier training sessions are included in all subscription setups without extra charge.
